Instant Noodles Recipe

Description

An recipe that every programmer needs to know

    Ingredients
  • 1 Package of Instant Noodles
  • 350 ml of Water
    Direction
  1. Start Boiling the Water and wait for 3-4 minutes
  2. Take off the noodles from the package and put it on the boiling water
  3. Put up the sauce that came in the package
  4. Stir for about 3 minutes
  5. Put anything that you want(for e.g Eggs)
  6. Eat it lol
